See our special recipes for Durga Puja

Bhog Khichuri

A sacred offering made from fragrant gobindobhog rice, moong dal, ghee, and mild spices. Served as part of the puja bhog, this wholesome dish is packed with tradition and flavor.

Labra

A traditional Bengali mixed vegetable dish made with pumpkin, potato, brinjal, and seasonal produce. Mildly spiced and often served alongside bhog khichuri during Durga Puja.

Chanar Payesh

A delightful Bengali dessert made with fresh paneer (chana), milk, and cardamom. Slow-cooked to creamy perfection and garnished with nuts—ideal for festive celebrations.
